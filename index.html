A little about who we are

 

More than just the best burger for the buck!

Steve's Classic Burgers was frst opened in 2015 by Steve Vough. He currently resides in Columbia, South Carolina.

Steve has a wide variety of interests from entrepreneurship to volunteering to education. And of course, cooking and food!

Faith and the community are important to Steve and his wife, best friend and co-owner, Marilynn.

They hope that you are able to join them at SCB to enjoy a family atmosphere and a great meal!
